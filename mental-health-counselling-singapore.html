Mental Health Counselling Singapore

Compassionate Care in Singapore

Access compassionate, professional counselling grounded in Singapore’s diverse culture. You’ll have a safe, confidential space to explore stressors, relationships, and life changes. Together, we focus on practical strategies that fit your daily routine and values.

Counsellors Who Understand Local Life

Work with qualified counsellors who understand local workplace pressures, academic expectations, and family dynamics. Their culturally sensitive approach respects multilingual backgrounds and community norms. You’ll be met with warmth, respect, and clear guidance tailored to you.

Convenient Options Across the Island

Choose in-person sessions near major MRT lines or meet online through secure video. Flexible scheduling, including evenings and weekends, helps support continuity. Wherever you are, you’ll experience consistent, structured sessions designed for comfort and progress.

Personalised Plans, Measurable Progress

Your goals shape every step, from the first intake to ongoing check-ins. Counsellors use evidence-informed methods to build coping skills, strengthen communication, and enhance emotional awareness. Regular reviews keep your plan adaptable as your needs evolve.

Start Your First Session with Confidence

Getting started is straightforward: contact the team, confirm your preferences, and book a time. Many services offer support in English, Mandarin, Malay, and Tamil for clearer communication. In your first session, you’ll set goals and boundaries in a private, respectful setting.

Frequently Asked Questions

What issues can Mental Health Counselling in Singapore help with, and what does a typical session involve?
Mental Health Counselling Singapore services support anxiety, stress and burnout, depression, relationship or family conflict, grief, trauma, and life transitions. A first session (about 50–60 minutes) covers your concerns, history, and goals; subsequent sessions use evidence-based approaches such as CBT, mindfulness, or solution-focused therapy. Sessions are confidential within legal limits and can be in-person or online.
How much does Mental Health Counselling in Singapore cost, and can I use subsidies or insurance?
Private counselling typically ranges from about S$80–S$250+ per session; public hospitals and community agencies may offer lower or means-tested fees. Depending on diagnosis and setting, subsidies via public institutions, CHAS, and limited MediSave use for approved chronic mental health care or inpatient psychiatric treatment may apply; some employers offer EAPs and some insurers cover outpatient mental health. Always confirm coverage and eligibility with the clinic and your insurer.
How do I choose a qualified counsellor in Singapore, and what if I’m in crisis?
Look for relevant credentials (e.g., SAC-registered counsellor, SPS-registered psychologist, or SMC-registered psychiatrist), experience with your concern, language fit, availability, and clear fees. Book via the provider’s online form or phone and consider a brief consult to assess fit. If you are in crisis, call Samaritans of Singapore at 1767, the IMH Mental Health Helpline at 6389 2222, or emergency services at 995/999 immediately.
